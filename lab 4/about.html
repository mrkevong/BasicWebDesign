About Collecting Watches

I will admit that I have a love for watches! I've enjoyed collecting watches since the age of 7. I am intrigued by the craftsmanship and the intricacy that goes into the creation of a watch.

My favorite brands of watches are Invicta, Casio (G-Shock), and of course Apple. Watches are just like money and time... you can never have enough.
